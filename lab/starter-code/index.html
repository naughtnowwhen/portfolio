About Me

I've love music for a long time. For some years i've been practicing drums obsessively after realizing that i wasn't near as good as i wanted to be and decided to pour myself into it. It took lots of time and effort but i have very much improved.


I see similarites in coding and meditating. Both things that i'd like to get much better at like how i did with drums and realize the path forward is to pour myself into them.


With meditation i was trying it by myself with reading/listening about it, but i think i truly began to learn the practice when i attended Dhamma Kunja, a wonderul place that i can't wait to go back to. It's very difficult, but one of the best things i've ever done.


with coding i'm drawn to typeScript and by extension C#
